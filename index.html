Genesys Aerosystems IDU Waypoint Loader

Pre-Flight Data Prep:
1. Paste rows from data software (Excel/Google Earth) or select a file.
2. Click "Load Data". Coordinates (DD, DDM, or DMS) will automatically normalize.
3. Click the appropriate button to export for your avionics or dispatch system.
