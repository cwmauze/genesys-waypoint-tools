Genesys Aerosystems IDU Waypoint Loader

How to use:
1. Select a file (.dat, .csv, .txt, or .kml) or paste rows from your master spreadsheet.
2. Click "Load Data" to populate the table.
3. Verify the waypoint data and click "Download USER.DAT" or "Export KML".
