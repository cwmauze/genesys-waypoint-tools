Genesys Aerosystems IDU Waypoint Loader

How to use:
1. Select a file (.dat, .wpt, .csv, .txt, .kml) or paste rows from your data software.
2. Click "Load Data" to populate the table. Garmin formats are automatically detected.
3. Verify information and click the descriptive download button for your specific avionics.
