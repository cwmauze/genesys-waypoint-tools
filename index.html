Genesys Aerosystems IDU Waypoint Loader

How to use:
1. Select a file (.dat, .wpt, .csv, .txt, .kml) or paste rows from your data software.
2. Click "Load Data" to populate the table. (Garmin CSV formats are auto-detected).
3. Click the desired export button to generate your flight planning data.
